SCIENCE OF NMN

Why We Need NMN: The NAD+ Collapse

The ₹8,000 imported NMN capsules are popular for a reason. Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide (NAD+) is critical for cellular energy and DNA repair. Unfortunately, our natural levels plummet as we age. By the time you reach 50, your NAD+ levels are approximately half of what they were in your twenties.

Recent 60-day randomized controlled trials with 80 healthy middle-aged participants demonstrated that supplementing with NMN significantly safely elevates NAD+ blood concentrations, effectively acting as a precursor to restore youthful cellular function.

AYURVEDA MEETS BIOGERONTOLOGY

Ashwagandha: Broad-Spectrum Efficacy

The Rasayana tradition was ahead of science by 3,000 years. Ashwagandha is no longer just a traditional remedy; it's a heavily researched adaptogen. Clinical reviews in 2025 highlight its multi-pathway benefits for healthspan.

Its primary mechanism involves lowering cortisol, which cascades into massive improvements in sleep quality, mental focus, and immune function. For the chronically stressed Indian professional, this is the foundational longevity supplement.

CLINICAL EVALUATION

Purified Shilajit: Beyond the Mountain Myth

Sourced from high mountain rocks, pure Shilajit is rich in fulvic acid. Randomized placebo-controlled evaluations published in major journals demonstrate it is a potent herbo-mineral compound.

It operates at the mitochondrial level, enhancing ATP production. Furthermore, clinical trials show significant improvements in male hormonal health, specifically free testosterone levels, and overall muscular strength endurance. Add purified Shilajit to the stack when you are serious about holistic hormonal and energetic balance.
